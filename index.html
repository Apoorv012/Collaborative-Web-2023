About us

Opencode is a transformative month-long program commencing each December, designed to ignite students' exploration into the dynamic realm of open source. Fueled by a passion for learning, the only prerequisite for participation is an enthusiastic heart. Our mission is to empower individuals with the tools and knowledge needed to contribute meaningfully to the open-source landscape.
